Primary Color
#01C16B (Vibrant Green): Represents growth, sustainability, and environmental responsibility.
Supporting Colors
Secondary Color
#FFCC00 (Golden Yellow): Highlights, alerts, or call-to-action elements like buttons. Conveys positivity and attention.
Accent Color
#007BFF (Sky Blue): Used for subtle accents, links, or non-critical buttons. Represents trust and cleanliness.
Neutral Colors
Light Gray: #F5F5F5 (Backgrounds or subtle borders).
Medium Gray: #D1D1D1 (Secondary text or icons).
Dark Gray: #11181C (Primary text or titles).
Alert Colors
Success
#4CAF50 (Muted Green): Indicates success or completed actions.
Warning
#FFA500 (Bright Orange): For warnings or pending actions.
Danger
#FF5A5F (Red): Used for errors or critical alerts.

Example Usage

Background and Text
Use #F5F5F5 as the background color.
Use #11181C for primary text and #D1D1D1 for secondary text.
Buttons
Primary button: #01C16B with white text.
Secondary button: #FFCC00 with dark gray text.
Icons
Active icons: #01C16B.
Inactive icons: #D1D1D1.
Alerts
Success: Background #4CAF50 with white text.
Warning: Background #FFA500 with dark gray text.
Danger: Background #FF5A5F with white text.
